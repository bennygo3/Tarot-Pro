About Xiamara

My name is Xiamara Chupaflor and I am a bruja of Apache/Comanche descent living in the Mexican border people's diaspora. I am a second-generation hustler, punk-lifer, queer, writer, mixed media collage artist and mediator. I am currently based in so called New Orleans, Louisiana occupied Chitimacha territory, Bulbancha. I was born to the West Texas desert, the Permian Basin, occupied Apache territory.

Since 2009 I have dedicated myself to honing my channeling skills through tarot divination and creating elaborate altars, intuiting my way through spells and candle magic using my tarot cards to guide my personal practices and create and maintain relationships with my ancestors, deities and spirit guides. I identify as a messenger, a seer and a conduit between the spirit world and the world we presently live in, with the gifts of clairaudience, clairsentience and claircognizance.

In addition, i have had the privilege of being a CNA for end of life care, a caregiver and educator for folks living with disabilities, a social worker, a resident caregiver in a long-term harm reduction-based housing project and mediator for my community. I also founded and created Brown Recluse Zine Distro, an online platform for Black and Brown writers to distribute their writing projects.

My perspectives and mission as a tarot guide are an extension of all of the skilled caregiving work that I have spent the better part of twenty years developing alongside my life experiences as a queer Brown woman living in the diaspora. I have been lucky that each path has led me to the next, my intuition and charisma lighting the way. Each job, project and community care endeavor taught me about magic and taught me a host of skills through repetition and ritual and being entrusted with people when they are their most vulnerable selves.

My practice is centered in transformative justice work through spiritual self-awareness and emotional regulation. My accumulated skill sets and experience have informed how I approach tarot: with compassion and honesty. I have a unique gift to create a bridge between a person and their personal unknowns. My desire is to carve out space for people's multiple truths and vulnerabilities, especially for those like myself, people who live at the intersections of structural violence.

I am a collage and mixed media artist, using only original clippings and media, choosing to work with non-digital media: paper, found objects, old print media and books, glitter, lace, fabric, herbs, scissors and glue. I thoroughly enjoy the methodical patience it takes to find images, objects, colors and other media to piece together a cohesive piece of work. My work often features esoteric ad mystic themes that resonate with my personal journey. Alongside tarot and a mixed media collage artist, I am a writer spending many years traveling with Brown Recluse Zine Distro speaking about DIY and punk vehicles of expression. All of my practices are based in the idea that you can turn trash into treasure.
