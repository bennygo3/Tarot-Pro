WELCOME TO CHUPAFLOR DIVINATION

A Space that Presents the Art and Mystical Musings of Xiamara Chupaflor

Tarot is one of the many tools we can use to navigate the numan experience. Tarot creates a road map for potentials and forges paths at the edge of the unknown. Tarot is my chosen tool to bridge spirit to the querent. My mission and my work are centered in connection. Connection with Spirit and connection with Self and to understand how to use your own brilliance and the tools available to you to create and achieve the movement you desire in life.

My offerings are simple, channeled tarot guidance and divination. i offer a variety of different structures for tarot guidance ranging from short and sweet three card pulls to more elaborate spreads that are more conducive for big life transitions or to compliment your deeper spiritual work.

I believe that healing should be accessible. I want to remove my offerings from the capitalist machine as much as possible. I offer sliding scale sessions and sponsored readings for QTIBIPOC. This means that if you fall under the QTIBIPOC umbrella and are having a financial hardship I will locate money for you to receive a reading that you don’t have to pay for. Everyone deserves access to healing and everyone needs to receive compensation for their gifts to survive in this world.

Also presented here is my collage art, mixed media art and other art collaborations as well as my writing about a variety of topics relating to studying, understanding and interpreting tarot and how it relates to a variety of topics relating to tarot, mysticism and manifestation, emotional regulation, spiritual and emotional health, decolonial and anti-capitalist politics and how all these concepts relate to modern magic and tarot.
